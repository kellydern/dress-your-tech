About Dress Your Tech

This 100 Day Project is a passion project by Boulder-based designer Kelly Dern. For 100 days, I created a different wallpaper design to dress your tech. All wallpapers are optimized for an iPhone 6 and are available to download and use on your device. Enjoy!

You can see my 2015 100 Day Project which explored 100 days of travel-inspired UI at wanderingwodering.co
