About Dress Your Tech

This 100 Day Project is a passion project by Boulder-based designer Kelly Dern (that's me!). For 100 days, I created a different wallpaper design to dress your tech. All wallpapers are optimized for mobile devices and are available to download (for free!) and use on your device. Enjoy!
